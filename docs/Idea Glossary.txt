# Idea Glossary

## Brand

The promises an organization consistently keeps.

---

## Story

The meaning people assign to your organization.

---

## Design

The intentional process of solving communication problems.

---

## Communication Debt

The accumulated cost of unclear, inconsistent, or neglected communication.

---

## Campaign

A coordinated effort designed to move people toward one outcome.

---

## Project

A collection of work supporting a campaign.

---

## Deliverable

A tangible asset produced by a project.

---

## Task

The smallest unit of work required to complete a deliverable.

---

## Preference vs Brand

Personal taste should never outweigh organizational clarity.

Brand becomes the referee.

---

## Change vs Polish

Polish improves execution.

Change improves direction.

Knowing the difference is leadership.

---

## Story Before Strategy

Before deciding what to communicate...

Decide what story people should experience.

---

## Systems Before Software

Healthy systems outlive technology.

Software should support the process—not define it.

---

## Design Solves Creative Problems

Great design begins long before fonts and colors.

It begins by asking better questions.

---

## Communication Is Hospitality

Every interaction communicates whether someone belongs.

Websites.

Signage.

Emails.

Social media.

Photography.

Typography.

All of it welcomes—or discourages.

---

## Every Deliverable Has One Job

One audience.

One message.

One action.

Confused communication is usually trying to accomplish too much.

---

## Creative Brief

A creative brief is not bureaucracy.

It is clarity before creativity.
