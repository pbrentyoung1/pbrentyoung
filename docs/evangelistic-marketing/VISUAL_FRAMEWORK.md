Visual Framework: The Journey to Transformation

Working Title

The Journey to the Well

↓

Design Intent

The visual should feel like a journey rather than a sales funnel. Avoid pyramids, conversion arrows, and language that makes people feel processed. The framework should communicate movement, relationship, and widening influence.

Possible Visual Metaphors

1\. A path moving toward and away from a well  
2\. Concentric ripples beginning at the well and reaching the town  
3\. A continuous line following the movement of John 4  
4\. A map from Samaria to the well to the city  
5\. A series of hand-drawn vessels, with the abandoned water jar marking transformation

Recommended Direction

Use the well as the central hinge. The first half moves from marketplace presence toward the personal encounter. The second half moves outward from transformation toward advocacy and community.

Left side:  
Marketplace → Person → Touchpoint → Pain → Trust

Center:  
Truth at the Well

Right side:  
Promise → Transformation → Advocacy → Community

Applications

• pbrentyoung.com series landing page  
• Evangelistic Marketing book spread  
• Creative learning module  
• Keynote and workshop slides  
• Social carousel  
• Printable discussion guide

Design Guardrails

• Human, warm, and editorial rather than corporate  
• No funnel imagery  
• No stock-photo aesthetic  
• Scripture remains visually primary  
• Marketing terms should be translated into human language  
• The framework should still make sense when printed in black and white
