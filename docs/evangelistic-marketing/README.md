Evangelistic Marketing: John 4 Series

Purpose

This directory contains the foundational thinking behind Evangelistic Marketing: Building a Church Brand That Resonates. It begins with Scripture, not with modern marketing theory. The goal is to observe how Jesus communicated, how people moved toward transformation, and what churches and mission-driven organizations can learn from those interactions.

Core Thesis

People do not arrive at transformation by accident. Jesus intentionally met people where they were, understood their real needs, built trust, revealed truth, invited response, and empowered transformed people to invite others.

Modern marketers describe this movement as a customer journey. In ministry, it is better understood as a journey toward transformation.

Primary Outputs

• A ten-part blog series for pbrentyoung.com  
• A central section of Evangelistic Marketing  
• A visual journey framework  
• A conference talk and workshop  
• A Creative learning module  
• Supporting podcast and video content

Editorial Principle

We are not forcing marketing concepts into Scripture. We are allowing timeless communication principles already demonstrated by Jesus to challenge the assumptions of modern church communication.
