Ministries are the organized expressions of ministry.

Youth, kids, Sunday services, Bible studies, groups, worship, hospitality, tech, care, outreach, and similar areas of work are ministries. They are how the church organizes and carries out the larger work of Ministry.

## In business

This is similar to the way an organization organizes its work into departments, teams, products, or service lines.

## In the church

Ministries help a church live out its mission in concrete, organized ways. A ministry is not automatically strategic just because it exists. A church may need to start, stop, combine, rename, strengthen, or clarify ministries when strategy reveals a better way to serve the mission.

## What remains true

Ministries serve the mission. They should not become monuments to familiarity, preference, or habit.
