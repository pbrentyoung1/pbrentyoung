---
term: Trust
principles: Brand builds trust
---

Trust is an emotion built on consistency and honesty: the confidence that you can predict how a person or organization will act in the future.

It grows where three things are present together. Empathy shows you understand what someone carries. Authority shows you are actually able to help. Consistency, repeated over time, proves that both are real and not a performance. Take any one away and trust thins: empathy without authority feels sincere but unable, authority without empathy feels capable but cold, and either one without consistency never gets the time it needs to form.

Trust creates room for deeper conversation, but it never entitles an organization to control the response. It is the currency every other term in this glossary spends.
