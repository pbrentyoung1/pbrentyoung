Story is the truthful narrative people encounter through our words, decisions, behaviors, and artifacts.

Story is not spin, invention, positioning, or a clever wrapper for something thin. Story is how people are wired to communicate and remember. People do not simply retain information, tactics, or announcements. They carry meaning through narrative. The work is not to manufacture a more attractive story. The work is to discover, steward, clarify, and consistently tell the true story of who God has called us to be.

## In business

Story helps people understand what something means, why it matters, and whether it belongs in the world they are trying to build.

## In the church

Story is the truthful narrative people experience as they encounter the church, its people, its ministries, its communication, and its care. A church does not invent its story. It stewards the story God has entrusted to it.

## What remains true

Story comes before strategy because people remember meaning before they remember tactics.
