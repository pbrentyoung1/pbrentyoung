Ministry is the whole work of the church as it lives out its calling, identity, and mission.

Ministry names the broad vocation of serving God and people. It is not one department, program, event, or activity. Those are ministries and ministry elements. Ministry is the larger work those expressions are meant to serve.

## In business

This is similar to the whole work of an organization before it is divided into departments, products, campaigns, or initiatives.

## In the church

Ministry is what the church is called to do: worship, witness, care, disciple, serve, gather, send, and embody the Gospel in a particular place and time.

## What remains true

Ministry is what we do. Ministries are how we organize and carry out that work.
