The accumulated cost of unclear, inconsistent, or neglected communication.

Like financial debt, it compounds. Every confusing announcement, outdated page, and contradictory signal borrows clarity from the future. Teams pay it back with interest, usually in the form of repeated questions, low trust, and rework.
